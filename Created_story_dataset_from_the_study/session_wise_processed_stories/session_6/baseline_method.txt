In the grim cityscape, Amy began to explore. She started to notice the dark forest and its many secrets. As Amy continued to explore, she descended into the unknown, eager to solve the mysteries. In a glade, she found a door. Its keyhole pulsated with mystic energy. Amy found the key and the door, when opened, transported her to a mysterious island. As Amy explored the island, she discovered a riddle for her to solve. The riddle spoke of truth, Amy read, her heart pulsing with the desire to solve the unsaid. Soon after, Amy solved the mystery. As a reward for solving the mystery, the island gave her a mask.
